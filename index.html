Biography

I am currently a PhD candidate in School of Computing at National University of Singapore, and fortunately supervised by Prof.Abhik Roychoudhury and Prof.Ilya Sergey. Previously, I studied as an exam-exempted master student in Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Prof.Yan Cai. I obtained my bachelor's degree from Tianjin University.


My research interests fall in program analysis and testing. The current research focuses on combining fuzzing and verification to expose security vulnerabilties in real-world complex software applications.
